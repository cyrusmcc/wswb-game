A game by Esther Perel

Where Should
We Begin?

A game of stories

Not about keeping score. The real reward lies in the meaningful conversations and deeper understanding that come from listening and telling stories together.

The goal

There's no winner. This game exists to spark meaningful stories, shared memories, and deeper understanding between two people.

Taking turns

  1. On your turn, draw a Prompt Card — it sets a broad theme.
  2. From your hand, secretly choose a Story Card that feels right. It shapes how you'll tell the story.
  3. Tell a story — preferably personal — inspired by both cards.
  4. Reveal your Story Card, then open the floor for questions and conversation.
  5. Pass the turn when you're done.

The cards

Prompt Cards are opening lines — "A risk I took that changed my life…" — they're a springboard, not a strict rule.

Story Cards give your story a flavour: funny, taboo, heartbreaking, nostalgic… You pick which fits best from your hand of 7.

Playing online

Player 1 creates the game and shares the code. Player 2 joins with that code. The game syncs automatically — no need to refresh.

Waiting for them…

Share your game code with your partner so they can join.
